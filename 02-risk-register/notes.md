
# Notes — Risk Register

**Judgment calls:**
- Defined the scales before scoring anything. Impact 5 = ">$1M loss OR regulatory
  breach OR >24h platform outage." Undefined scales are the #1 weakness in example
  registers online — scores without criteria are vibes.
- Assigned every risk a BUSINESS owner (Eng Lead, Sales Ops, CTO), not "security."
  The business owns risk; security facilitates. This is also what makes treatment
  deadlines enforceable.
- Deliberately ACCEPTED one risk (R-15, physical security) with sign-off and an annual
  review date. A register where everything is "Mitigate" hasn't engaged with risk
  appetite; documented acceptance is a feature, not a failure.
- Showed inherent AND residual scores so the value of each treatment is visible.

**What I'd do differently:** add a velocity/trend column — a static score hides
whether a risk is improving or deteriorating between reviews.
